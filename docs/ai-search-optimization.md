# AI Search Optimization

AI Search Optimization is the process of structuring and optimizing web content so that artificial intelligence systems can discover, interpret and cite it as a trusted source.

AI search systems include:

- ChatGPT
- Google AI Overviews
- Microsoft Copilot
- Perplexity AI
- Google Gemini

## Key components

- Entity clarity
- Structured data (JSON-LD)
- Topical authority
- Answer engine optimization
- Knowledge graph integration
